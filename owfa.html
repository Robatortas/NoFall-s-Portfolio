Other Words for Alone | OWFA

2D tile-based video game with procedural terrain generation and inhouse game engine

Other Words for Alone is a 2 year long project meant to hone my skills as a programmer. In the latest alpha versions of the game made with our own inhouse game engine made entirely in Java without the use of any external libraries. we implemented perlin noise-based terrain generation, physics, collision, inventory system, player movement, stamina, health and other interactuable items and entities.
